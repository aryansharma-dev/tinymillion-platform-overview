# User Personas

## 1. Customer
- Discovers products via creator links
- Shops from creator mini-stores
- Uses AI chatbot for queries
- Tracks orders and deliveries

## 2. Seller / Creator
- Owns a personalised mini-store
- Manages products, pricing, inventory
- Views analytics and payouts
- Uses AI tools for content, ads, and growth

## 3. Platform Admin
- Seller onboarding & verification
- Product approvals
- Commission & payouts
- Dispute resolution & fraud checks
